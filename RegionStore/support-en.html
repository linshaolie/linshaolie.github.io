üìñ How to Use

1. Browse Regions

  • Open the app to see all available regions organized by continent
  • Scroll through the list to explore different countries
  • Each region card displays the country flag and name

2. Search for Regions

  • Tap the search bar at the top of the screen
  • Type the country name in English or Chinese
  • Results will appear instantly as you type

3. Add to Favorites

  • Tap the star icon on any region card to add it to favorites
  • Access your favorites from the "Favorites" tab
  • Quickly browse your most-used regions

4. Open App Store

  • Tap any region card to open that region's App Store
  • Choose between in-app browser or native App Store in Settings
  • Explore region-specific apps and content

5. Customize Settings

  • Go to the "Settings" tab
  • Select your preferred theme (Vibrant Orange or Technology Blue)
  • Choose how to open App Store links

Q: Can I download apps from different regions?

To download apps from a specific region, you need to sign in to the App Store with an Apple ID registered in that region. Region Store helps you browse and discover apps, but downloads are handled by the official App Store.

Q: Why can't I see some countries?

Region Store includes all major countries and regions where the App Store is available. If you can't find a specific country, it may not have an official App Store presence.

Q: Is the app free?

Yes, Region Store is completely free to download and use with no in-app purchases or subscriptions.

Q: Does the app require an internet connection?

Yes, an internet connection is required to browse App Store content from different regions.
